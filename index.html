Our Story
Why Ushered exists
👴 Dadaji
"He always said he was fine."
My grandfather lived with high blood pressure for over 10 years. He knew about it. His doctors told him. His family told him. But he always believed he was okay — that he didn't need the medications, didn't need the checkups, didn't need to worry.

That denial cost him his life.

One year later, my grandmother started showing the same signs. But this time, our family stepped in. We advocated for her. She is alive today because someone refused to let her say she was fine when she wasn't.
The difference between them?
Someone advocated for her.
That one difference — between loss and survival — is why I built Ushered.
👦

From San Jose, CA. Taught myself to code this summer. Built this for my grandfather, my grandmother, and every family that deserves a second chance.
